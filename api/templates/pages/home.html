About me.

I'm John! I'm a 3/4 Computer Science major at Stevens Institute of Technology in Hoboken, NJ.

Programming is one of my greatest passions; I am always looking for ways to further develop my skills and branch out of my comfort zone. My main area of interest is currently systems and low-level programming, and recently I have started exploring iOS app development.

My coursework, self-study, and work experience has helped me develop a broad skillset. My coursework has included data structures and algorithms, systems, computer architecture, and PL theory. In the Spring 2023 semester, I started working as a Course Assistant for CS 115 (Intro to CompSci), which has helped with my debugging and communication skills.

Languages I have learned and am proficient in include Python, C, ARM assembly, Java, and OCaml. I recently learned Swift on my own time, and used it to develop my first major project, SimpleHW.
